# GoGroup

## English

> GoGroup is an operational holding company focused on building its own businesses and partnering with others to create outstanding digital products. It was started in 2019 by Sascha Weiler, Nico Weiler and Shishir Goel, building on many years of experience as serial entrepreneurs. Its offices are in Berlin, London, Kathmandu and Noida with over 100 employees.

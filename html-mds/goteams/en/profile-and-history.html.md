# GoTeams - Profile and History

## About GoTeams

GoTeams operates modern, scalable tech teams for high-growth digital products. Based in Berlin, Germany and Noida, India, our team of over 100 specialists works across multiple technology domains to support organizations in delivering exceptional digital products.

---

## A Part of GoGroup

GoTeams is part of GoGroup, a Berlin, Germany based platform for bringing ambitious digital ideas to life across industries and growing them sustainably.

---

### GoGroup's Mission

The GoGroup follows its mission through three key activities:
- **Incubating own ventures** - Building and launching new digital businesses.
- **Partnering with entrepreneurs and existing companies** - Collaborating to accelerate growth.
- **Investing in the movers and shakers of tomorrow** - Supporting promising ventures and innovators.

---

## How it Started

The roots of GoTeams go back to 2015 when brothers **Nico Weiler** and **Sascha Weiler**, together with **Shishir Goel**, were looking for a scalable way to power tech and product for their own entrepreneurial endeavors.

---

### The Key Insight

What they realized: the most pressing bottleneck for digital businesses is not the lack of ideas, sales, or online marketing, but the lack of substantial tech capabilities.

---

### Building the Foundation

They built a tech powerhouse in India - first to empower their own ventures, and later to help third parties grow theirs. Today, it has grown into the backbone of great companies and products, becoming a home for excellent engineers.

## Business Model

GoTeams provides a unique approach to team building:
- Determine exact technical requirements
- Hire developers full-time through local entities
- Operate teams on behalf of partners on a long-term basis

This model ensures dedicated, scalable teams that work as an extension of our partners organizations.
